High Performance Computing Resources

NERSC Allocation

Through the DOE Mission Science program, the Molecular Foundry has received an allocation of computing resources at NERSC (National Energy Research Scientific Computing Center), a large scale super-computer facility co-located at Lawrence Berkeley National Lab. If you need computing resources for your user project research, please contact the Foundry staff responsible for NERSC sub-allocations: Edward Barnard and David Prendergast

If you use NERSC resources during your user project in 2024 please acknowledge NERSC in your publications of work resulting from the use of NERSC resources:

"This research used resources of the National Energy Research Scientific Computing Center, a DOE Office of Science User Facility supported by the Office of Science of the U.S. Department of Energy under Contract No. DE-AC02-05CH11231 using NERSC award BES-ERCAP0028035."

Berkeley Lab Resources

Berkeley Lab's Science IT supports scientists by providing computing services including the Lawerencium cluster and on-premises data storage.
